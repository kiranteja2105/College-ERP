Courses

MPC(JEE Mains & Advance)

Sunshine has made a remarkable mark in the history of Teaching for MPC (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry) students, shaping the futures of thousands of aspiring engineers and technologists more than a decade. With a unique and personalized approach, the teaching at Sunshine for MPC students is dedicated to being concept-driven, offering a specially designed curriculum to each student, and focusing on care and compassion. Students benefit from a customized learning experience, increasing their chances of success in the highly challenging Joint Entrance Examination (JEE).

Courses Offered By MPC

  • V JOI [JEE Main - ADVANCED] AC Class Room
  • JOI [JEE Main - ADVANCED] AC Class Room
  • SPL [JEE - MAIN]
  • IC - BATCH [EAPCET]

BiPC(NEET & EAPCET)

Sunshine has emerged as a leading Institution in the teaching landscape for BIPC (Biology, Physics, Chemistry) students, marking its presence with a legacy of more than a decade. This institute has played a pivotal role in shaping the aspirations and careers of numerous aspiring medical professionals. The curriculum is meticulously crafted to ensure that students grasp the fundamental concepts thoroughly, which is crucial for success in exams like the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET). This deep understanding not only helps students perform well in exams but also prepares them for the practical application of these concepts in the medical profession.

Courses Offered By BiPC

  • V JAIN ALPHA [NEET] AC Class Room
  • ALPHA [NEET] AC Class Room
  • BEETA [NEET]
  • IC - BATCH [EAPCET]
